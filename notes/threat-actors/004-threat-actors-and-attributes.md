# 2.1 Threat Actors and Attributes

## Goal

Compare and contrast common threat actors and motivations.

## Threat Actors

- **Nation-state**
- **Unskilled attacker**
- **Hacktivist**
- **Insider threat**
- **Organized crime**
- **Shadow IT**

## Attributes of Actors

- **Internal vs. external**
- **Resources and funding**
- **Sophistication and capability**

## Quick Review

- Actor type tells you *who* is likely attacking.
- Attributes help estimate attacker capability and likely methods.
