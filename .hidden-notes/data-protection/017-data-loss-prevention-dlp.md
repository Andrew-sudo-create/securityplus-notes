Data Loss Prevention (DLP) - Tools and policies that detect and prevent sensitive data from leaving approved boundaries.

What DLP can do:
- inspect data in motion, at rest, and in use
- identify sensitive content by pattern and classification
- block, quarantine, encrypt, or alert on policy violations

Common DLP channels:
- email
- cloud uploads
- web traffic
- endpoints and removable media

Main objective:
- stop accidental or unauthorized data exfiltration
