Configuring a DLP - Building practical rules and workflows to protect sensitive data.

Typical setup steps:
1. Identify sensitive data categories.
2. Create detection rules (keywords, regex, fingerprints, labels).
3. Define policy actions (block, warn, encrypt, notify).
4. Apply policies to channels (email, endpoint, cloud, web).
5. Run in monitor mode first to reduce false positives.
6. Tune rules and exceptions based on real usage.

Operational best practices:
- align policies with business and compliance requirements
- review alerts regularly
- update rules as data types and threats evolve
