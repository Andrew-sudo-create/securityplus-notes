# 2.2 Human Vectors and Social Engineering

## Goal

Explain common threat vectors and attack surfaces.

## Human Vectors / Social Engineering Techniques

- phishing
- vishing
- smishing
- misinformation / disinformation
- impersonation
- business email compromise
- pretexting
- watering hole
- brand impersonation
- typosquatting

## Quick Review

- Social engineering attacks target people, not just systems.
- They often appear as legitimate communication or trusted brands.
